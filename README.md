## 📋 Overview

This repository contains the complete SEO content strategy for **Financial Protection Advocate** — a licensed non-life insurance agent brand in the Philippines specializing in health insurance comparison and consultation.

### Target Audience
- Filipino professionals aged 25–50
- Self-employed, freelancers, OFW families, SME owners
- First-time health coverage buyers
- People switching from HMO to health insurance

### Providers Covered
Pacific Cross, MediCard, Oona Insurance, PhilCare, Etiqa, ValuCare, IMS Wellthcare, iCare, Cocolife, and Keystone

## 🎯 Core Buyer Pain Points (Research-Based)

1. "My premium jumped 50% after I filed a claim"
2. "My pre-existing condition isn't covered — they just told me now"
3. "HMOs get expensive as I age — and I have to renew every year"
4. "I don't know the difference between HMO and health insurance"
5. "I can't find a doctor in their network near me"
6. "The claims process is slow — I had to pay out of pocket first"
7. "I'm not employed anymore — I lost my company HMO"
