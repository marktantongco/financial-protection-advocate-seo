## 🎯 Core Buyer Pain Points

### Pain Point #1: Premium Loading
> "My premium jumped 50% after I filed a claim"

**Context:** Pacific Cross and some insurers practice premium loading after claims.

**Content Angle:** Explain why it happens, which providers do it, and how to prepare.

---

### Pain Point #2: Pre-Existing Condition Exclusions
> "My pre-existing condition isn't covered — they just told me now"

**Context:** Waiting periods (6–12 months) and disclosure requirements are misunderstood.

**Content Angle:** Educate on waiting periods and disclosure best practices.

---

### Pain Point #3: HMO Age-Based Pricing
> "HMOs get expensive as I age — and I have to renew every year"

**Context:** HMOs are prepaid, renewed annually, premiums increase with age.

**Content Angle:** Position health insurance as the long-term alternative.

---

### Pain Point #4: HMO vs Insurance Confusion
> "I don't know the difference between HMO and health insurance"

**Context:** Most Filipinos use these terms interchangeably.

**Content Angle:** Create clear comparison content with tables and plain language.

---

### Pain Point #5: Network Accessibility
> "I can't find a doctor in their network near me"

**Context:** Provider networks vary from 1,600 to 30,000+ doctors.

**Content Angle:** Include network size comparisons in all provider content.

---

### Pain Point #6: Claims Process Delays
> "The claims process is slow — I had to pay out of pocket first"

**Context:** Reimbursement claims can take weeks; LOA has specific requirements.

**Content Angle:** Provide step-by-step claims guides per provider.

---

### Pain Point #7: Employment Transition Gaps
> "I'm not employed anymore — I lost my company HMO"

**Context:** Corporate HMOs don't follow employees when they leave.

**Content Angle:** Target career shifters with transition guides.
