## Post Structure

### H1 Title
```
[PRIMARY KEYWORD] — [COMPELLING HOOK]
```

**Example:** Pacific Cross Health Insurance Philippines Review: Is It Worth It for Self-Employed Filipinos?

---

### Quick Answer Box (40-60 words)
```
In short: [Direct answer in 2 sentences]. [One key caveat].
```

**Example:**
> In short: Pacific Cross offers comprehensive health insurance with flexible plans for self-employed Filipinos, including the popular FlexiShield series. However, be aware of premium loading after claims and strict pre-existing condition waiting periods.

---

### Introduction (100 words)
```
- Open with a direct answer to the primary search intent
- Hook the reader with a real pain point from Master Context
- Preview what the article will cover
- Include primary keyword naturally in first 100 words
```

---

### H2 Section 1: What is [PROVIDER/TOPIC]?
```
- Plain language explanation
- Define key terms
- Establish expertise
- Include 2-3 semantic seed terms
- Word count: 200-300 words
```

---

### H2 Section 2: Who It's Best For (And Who Should Skip It)
```
- Create a "Best For" list
- Create a "Skip It If" list
- Address specific audience segments
- Word count: 200-300 words
```

---

### H2 Section 3: Key Benefits
```
- Use comparison table if comparing 2+ providers
- Include data points with years
- Address network size, pricing, coverage
- Word count: 300-400 words
```

**Comparison Table Format:**
| Feature | Provider A | Provider B |
|---------|-----------|-----------|
| Network Size | X doctors | Y doctors |
| Annual Limit | ₱X | ₱Y |
| Pre-existing Condition | X months waiting | Y months waiting |

---

### H2 Section 4: Common Concerns + Honest Answers
```
- Address 3-5 buyer pain points from Master Context
- Use Q&A format for readability
- Be honest about limitations
- Word count: 200-300 words
```

---

### H2 Section 5: Lessons Learned from Real Buyers
```
- Share anonymized buyer experiences
- Include specific scenarios
- Provide actionable takeaways
- Word count: 150-250 words
```

---

### H2 Section 6: FAQ (5+ Questions)
```
- Format as FAQPage schema-ready Q&A
- Include People Also Ask questions
- Keep answers 40-60 words each
```

**Format:**
```
**Q: [Question]?**
A: [40-60 word answer]
```

---

### CTA (Soft Close)
```
- Invite DM or free consultation
- IC Philippines compliant
- No aggressive sales language
- 50-75 words
```

**Example:**
> Ready to find the right health coverage for your situation? As an IC Philippines licensed agent, I can help you compare options from Pacific Cross, MediCard, PhilCare, and other top providers — with no pressure to buy. DM me "PROTECT" on Facebook or Instagram for a free consultation.

---

## Meta Description Template
```
[Primary keyword] + [key benefit] + [CTA]. Under 155 characters.
```

**Example:**
> Pacific Cross health insurance Philippines review: plans, pricing, network size, and honest pros/cons for self-employed Filipinos. Get a free quote today.
